## How to Use

### Navigation
1. **Click any section** in the left sidebar
2. The section **highlights in purple** when active
3. The right content area **updates immediately**
4. The sidebar **stays visible** as you scroll (sticky)

### Reading Data
- **Labels** are on the left (gray text)
- **Values** are on the right (dark text)
- **Subsection titles** group related data
- **Source links** show document references

### Mobile View
- Sidebar moves to top on small screens
- Navigation becomes horizontal scroll
- Data displays in single column
